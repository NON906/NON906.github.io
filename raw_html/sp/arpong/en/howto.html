ArPong Help Site

How to play

Preparation

  1. Download and print following (from such as PC). And make the marker box.

    (URL: http://mumei.rosx.net/sp/arpong/ArBox.pdf)

    Download (pdf)

  2. Tap Start button from title. And select Player1 or 2.
  3. (Only Player2) Select Player1's phone or tablet.
  4. According to the screen, project the marker box with your device's camera.
    (If camera's image is blurred and unresponsive, push Focus button to adjust focus.)
    (If you show "Please project this marker." again, project the marker box again.)
  5. If you don't find your racket, let go a little out of the marker box.

How to play

You move your device, your racket move together.
Hit the ball with your racket.
If you want to end this game, push your device's Back button.
